Superman

Powers and Abillites

  • Superhuman strength
    • Superhuman speed
  • Superhuman vision (including X-ray, microscopic, telescopic, and infrared)
  • Superhuman hearing
  • Invulnerability
  • Heat vision
  • Flight
  • Super breath (also freeze breath)

Superman is the sole survivor of the planet Krypton. His father, Jor-El, discovered that a nuclear chain reaction was building inside Krypton that would soon shatter the entire world so he took his son, put him in a starcraft and launched the starcraft toward Earth just before Krypton exploded.

Daredevil

Powers and Abillites

  • Blind
  • Superhuman Accuracy
  • Superhuman Sensitivity

Daredevil's identity in the real world is Matt Murdock, a lawyer living in the Hell's Kitchen neighborhood in New York. When he was younger, he was blinded by a radioactive substance that fell from an oncoming vehicle, and even though he lost his sight, his other four senses were heightened to a superhuman level.
